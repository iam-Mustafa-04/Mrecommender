Bajirao Mastani
This period romantic-drama traces the epic love saga of Maratha chieftain Bajirao and princess Mastani, who leaves home and arrives in her lover's land, only to face rejection by his family. While Mastani fights for her safety, pride and love, Bajirao strives hard to strike a balance between his love for Mastani and his respect for his first wife, Kashi Bai. Eventually, politics from scheming family members drives the two apart, resulting in a tragic end. Watch it for: Priyanka Chopra's touching portrayal of the devoted and lovelorn Kashi Bai, and the chilling climax where the star-cross lovers breath their last.
